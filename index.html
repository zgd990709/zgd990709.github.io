Macrocyclic and Supramolecular Chemistry Lab

   Research in MASCL integrates synthetic and physical organic chemistry with materials and life sciences. The main interest is the development of innovative macrocyclic and supramolecular chemistry.
  In particular, we focus on the design and synthesis of novel and functional macrocycles which are capable of serving as privileged platform molecules and the study of molecular recognition and self-assembly, anion-π interactions, chiroptical materials and supramole-cular asymmetric catalysis, organometallic reactions and functional systems.
